After defeating Moonlord, The Moon begins to crumble, Its up to you to destroy the moon before It destroys terraria itself. This mod currently in the open alpha stage it currently adds 1 weapon 2 blocks and 3 crafting stations a set of armor, wings and 1 bar! Note if after moonlord it does not say that the gods are angry it means that your world is glitched and you need a new world.
